CORONAVIRUS UPDATE

The WFMA Migrant support centre is CLOSED due to the Coronavirus.

We are in the process of setting up a remote service.

If you need help, please text your full name to 07496394175, and one of our volunteers will ring you back.

Please do not call this number as there is no-one to answer it.

For information on how you can support migrants during the pandemic, read the Covid-19 Mutual Aid UK: Guide for Supporting Migrants During Coronavirus.
